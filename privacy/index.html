Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This site is simple: a calculator, some articles, and no user accounts. We don't ask for your email, we don't store your inputs, and we don't know who you are. Here's the full picture.

What we collect

Nothing we store ourselves. All calculator inputs — your age, savings, monthly contributions — exist only in your browser. They are never transmitted to any server we control. Close the tab and they're gone.

Basic analytics. We use standard analytics to understand aggregate traffic — which pages are visited, how long people stay, what devices they use. This data is anonymized and used only to improve the site. No individual user profiles are built.

Third-party services

Google AdSense. This site displays ads served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your browsing history across sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads, or visit optout.aboutads.info for broader opt-out options.

Google Fonts. Fonts are loaded from Google's servers. This means your browser makes a request to Google when you visit the site. Google's privacy policy governs that request.

Cookies

We don't set any first-party cookies. Third-party services (Google Ads, analytics) may set their own cookies as described above. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time — the calculator will still work fine without them.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Links to other sites

Blog articles may link to external sites. Once you leave this site, their privacy policies apply, not ours. We don't control what third parties do with your data.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect it. We don't send email notifications because we don't have your email.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at hello@whenwillimakeamillion.com.
